Get the medical products you need, delivered.

The Procedure

You find the product that you need on our platform

You chose the products that you need and order them.

After you order application will determine the closest and most convenient pharmacy from you in order to initate the delivery procedure.

One of our drones departs to the pharmacy and collects the products that you need.

Our drone delivers the products to your doorstep, after scanning a QR code you confirm the succesful order and the payment gets done.
